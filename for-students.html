Interested in Joining Us?
Applications for Fall 2020 are now closed. We encourage you to consider pitching a project this semester instead. If you'd prefer to just apply as an analyst, please check back in January 10, 2021, when applications for new analysts will go live.
Pitch a Policy Project Instead!
BIP is seeking a new policy project relating to health policy, education policy, or urban policy. The successful project pitcher will join our executive board in January 2021 and will work with our research director to formalize the project's timeline, goals, and partners ahead of building a research team for September 2021.

We are looking for students who are passionate about contributing to finding solutions to issues they see in the Rhode Island community. If you are a creative-thinker, solution-oriented, and passionate about community building, we encourage you to consider pitching a project.
